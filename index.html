OneMSU: Campus Mobile Consolidation

MSU students were juggling 5+ separate campus apps just to check dining hours, find parking, and access safety features. Through interviews with 8 students, I found that 68% abandoned tasks because they couldn't figure out which app had what they needed. I redesigned the information architecture around how students actually think about their tasks‚Äînot how IT departments organize systems‚Äîand made safety features accessible from any screen instead of buried in a separate app.
